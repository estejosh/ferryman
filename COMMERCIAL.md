# Commercial Licensing

Ferryman is source-available under the [Ferryman Source-Available License](LICENSE).
This page explains when you need a paid license and how to get one.

## When Ferryman is free

- **Any non-production use** — evaluation, development, testing, personal projects — is free, with no Seat limit.
- **Production use up to 3 Seats** is free. A **Seat** is a distinct human who operates, administers, deploys, or accesses Ferryman or its outputs. Automated agents/workers don't count as Seats themselves — they roll up to the human they act for.

## When you need a commercial license

- **Production use beyond 3 Seats** requires a paid, **per-Seat** commercial license.
- Licensing is per additional Seat above the free three. You license the Seats you actually use in production.
